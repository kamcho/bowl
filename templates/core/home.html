Game types

Three sanctioned divisions run on the same oil pattern. Pick one format per bowler; doubles and trios use team scoring with individual averages on file for handicap where posted.

    Singles

    Solo · scratch & handicap flights

    You bowl your own six-game block. Series pinfall ranks you in scratch and handicap brackets (90% of 220, max 30 pins). Awards by division; finals for top eight in each flight.

    Partner up · team series

    Two bowlers share a pair; four Baker-style team games plus two regular team games (six total on the scorecard). Team total pinfall seeds the bracket. Substitutes allowed before Week 1 only.

Help desk

FAQ

Quick answers for bowlers entering the Metro Open. Full rules live in the rulebook at check-in.

Can I bowl more than one format?

Yes — you may enter singles plus one doubles or one trios team. You cannot roster on two doubles or two trios squads.

How does handicap work?

Singles handicap is 90% of the difference between your entering average and 220, capped at 30 pins per game. Doubles and trios use combined team entering averages unless you declare scratch only at registration.

What if I need a substitute?

Doubles and trios may replace a rostered bowler before Week 1 only, with TD approval and a $15 admin fee. No subs once bracket play begins.

Are refunds available?

Full refund if you withdraw seven or more days before your first block. Within seven days, credit toward a future Metro Lanes event only — no cash refunds.

What equipment is allowed?

USBC equipment rules apply. No balance holes; thumb slugs and interchangeable thumbs OK. Inspectors may spot-check before finals.
